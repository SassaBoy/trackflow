1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, information collected automatically when you use our services, and information received from third-party carriers and partners.

Information you provide:

  • Name, email address, and phone number when you create an account or submit a support request
  • Shipping and billing addresses associated with your orders
  • Tracking numbers and order identifiers you enter into our platform
  • Communications you send us via support forms, live chat, or email
  • Return request details including order numbers, item descriptions, and stated reasons

Information collected automatically:

  • IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers
  • Pages visited, time spent, clicks, and navigation paths within our platform
  • Referral URLs and search terms that led you to our site
  • Cookies and similar local storage technologies (see Section 4)

Information from third parties:

  • Shipment status updates, scan events, and delivery confirmations from DHL, FedEx, UPS, and other integrated carriers
  • Customs and border protection event data associated with your shipment

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to operate, improve, and personalise our services:

  • Provide real-time shipment tracking and status updates
  • Process return requests and issue refunds
  • Send transactional notifications including tracking alerts, delivery confirmations, and support replies
  • Respond to your customer support enquiries
  • Detect, prevent, and investigate fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • Analyse aggregate usage patterns to improve our platform and user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations, court orders, and lawful government requests

We do not use your personal information for automated decision-making that produces significant legal effects without human oversight.

3. Information Sharing

TrackFlow does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for their marketing purposes. We share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Carrier partners: We share tracking identifiers and necessary contact details with DHL and other carriers solely to facilitate delivery and resolve shipment issues
  • Service providers: We engage trusted vendors (hosting, analytics, email delivery, payment processing) who process data on our behalf under strict data processing agreements
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information when required by law, regulation, or valid legal process, including from customs and border protection authorities in connection with international shipments
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred to the successor entity, with advance notice provided to you
  • With your consent: We may share information for any other purpose with your explicit prior consent

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enable core platform functionality, remember your preferences, and analyse how our services are used.

  • Essential cookies: Required for the platform to function (session management, security). Cannot be disabled.
  • Functional cookies: Remember your preferences such as language, saved tracking numbers, and inbox messages stored locally in your browser.
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand aggregate usage patterns. We use privacy-preserving analytics and do not build individual behavioural profiles.

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling non-essential cookies will not affect your ability to use core tracking features.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy or as required by law:

  • Account data is retained for the duration of your account plus 3 years after closure
  • Shipment tracking records are retained for 5 years to support returns, disputes, and regulatory compliance
  • Support communications are retained for 2 years
  • Analytics data is anonymised and aggregated after 13 months

You may request deletion of your personal data at any time (see Section 6).

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten"), subject to legal retention requirements
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data for direct marketing or based on our legitimate interests
  • Restriction: Request restriction of processing in certain circumstances
  • Withdraw consent: Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, contact our privacy team at privacy@trackflow.com. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

7. International Data Transfers

TrackFlow operates globally and your shipment data may be processed in countries other than your own — including the United States, European Union member states, and South Africa — in order to track your parcel through international carrier networks. We ensure that all international transfers are protected by appropriate safeguards, including Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission and equivalent mechanisms where applicable.

8. Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organisational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These include 256-bit TLS encryption for all data in transit, encrypted storage for data at rest, access controls and role-based permissions, regular security audits and penetration testing, and incident response procedures.

No method of internet transmission is 100% secure. If you become aware of a security issue, please contact security@trackflow.com immediately.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date and, where appropriate, by sending an email notification. Your continued use of our services after changes take effect constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Privacy questions?

Our Data Privacy Officer is available to assist with any concerns or data requests.
